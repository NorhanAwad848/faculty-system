Academic Programs


The college awards the degree:


(1) Bachelor's degree in computers and information:
1- Bachelor of Computer Science program
2- Bachelor of Information Systems program
3- Bachelor of Information Technology program
4- Bachelor's Program in Software Engineering (with fees)

(2) Postgraduate diplomas specialized in computers and information:
1 - Diploma Program in Computer Science
2 - Diploma in Information Systems
3 - Information Technology Diploma Program

(3) Applied Postgraduate Diploma in Computers and Information:
1 - Diploma in Geographical Information Systems.
2 - Diploma in Medical and Bioinformatics program.
3 - Diploma in Business Information Technology.

(4) Master's degree in computers and information: -
1 - Master's Program in Computer Science
2 - Master's Program in Information Systems
3-Master's Program in Information Technology
4 - Master's Program in Geographical Information Systems
5 - Master's Program in Medical and Bioinformatics
6 - Master's Program in Business Information Technology

(5) Doctorate of Philosophy in Computers and Information:
1 - Ph.D. Program in Computer Science.
2 - Ph.D. program in information systems.
3 - Ph.D. Program in Information Technology
